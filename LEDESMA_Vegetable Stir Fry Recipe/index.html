Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch
  • Optional: cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the vegetables by washing them thoroughly and cutting them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch to make the sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet, and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Pour the sauce over the vegetables in the skillet, and toss to coat evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Remove from heat and serve the vegetable stir fry immediately, optionally over cooked rice or noodles.
